Driving change by design: 25 years at the forefront of global impact brand innovation

From shaping visual narratives for international NGOs to leading digital transformation at the OECD/ITF, my ambition is to make a meaningful difference through strategic creativity and communications design

My mission

For over 20 years, I’ve worked for global NGOs, charities and innovative enterprises – focusing on social justice and environmental sustainability causes.

I've carved out a niche, by adapting my extensive commercial design and branding expertise into a toolkit to support international development and diplomacy.

I now guide design transformation at the OECD’s International Transport Forum and am committed to continue contributing to purpose-led communications to drive influence and make meaningful progress in the sectors that are important to me.
