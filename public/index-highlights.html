Art directing the world’s foremost transport summit

Every year the ITF hosts the world’s summit for nearly 100 ministers of transport and up to another 1,400 guests from around the world. Such an event demands an exceptionally large number of designed assets. The 2023 summit creative branding and design management was entirely my responsibility.

Distilling the essence of global health financing innovation

MedAccess provides advanced volume guarantees and logistics underwriting to the World’s biggest medical supply and pharmaceutical companies. Their brand was unfocused and their brand guidelines were only a few pages of basic detail. They hired me to change that.

Annual performance reporting suite

While I was Creative Design Officer at IPPF’s London HQ, one of the most important projects I delivered was the art direction for their annual report. In the end, I designed the global report from 2011 to 2013. The 2012—13 edition here is the last edition I produced.

Promoting satellite-based water quality testing for the developing world

PrimeWater is an EU Horizon2020-funded research project that generates information on the effects of upstream changes on future water quality and quantity. Building on advanced Earth-Observation data products, integration with additional data sources and diagnostic modelling tools, public and private sector decisions for water resources management are provided with better and actionable information.
